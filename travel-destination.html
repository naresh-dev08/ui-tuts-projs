Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, lush rice terraces, and vibrant culture. It's the perfect destination for relaxation and adventure!

Activities to Do

  • Visit the Ubud Monkey Forest
  • Relax on Seminyak Beach
  • Explore the rice terraces in Tegalalang
  • Take a yoga class in Ubud
  • Snorkel at Menjangan Island

Travel Tips

  1. Always carry cash, as many places do not accept credit cards.
  2. Respect local customs and traditions.
  3. Stay hydrated and wear sunscreen.
  4. Negotiate prices at markets.
  5. Be cautious while riding scooters.
