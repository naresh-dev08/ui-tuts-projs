Spaghetti Bolognese

This is a classic Italian pasta dish with a rich and hearty meat sauce. Perfect for a family dinner!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g minced meat
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and saut√© the onions and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the minced beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes and tomato paste. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.
